About Us

Astopia

Astopias a portmanteau of the Greek ástron (ἄστρον), meaning “star,” and the English topia, from the Greek topos (τόπος), meaning “place.” Literally, “a place of stars.”Astopia is the guiding philosophy and aspirational goal of the student union. Us, Astopia, defines ourselves not just as a service provider, but as the heart of a vibrant, supportive, and stellar community. We promise to strive the best for our students

恆曦

恆  指恆心，代表著我們會堅持不懈為學生服務和為學生創造更好校園環境的承諾，也象徵著穩定性和可靠性。
曦  指晨光，代表著希望、光明、溫暖和活力。象徵著為校園帶來新的氣象、溫暖的關懷，以及如晨光般驅散黑暗、指引方向的領導力。
